Contact

Found a bug in one of our projects?

Found a broken link or something that doesn't work as you expect it to? You can report issues in our bug tracker, or let us know on IRC or by email. Contact details are provided below. Please include details such as what happened, what you expected to happen, and the URLs of any pages involved.

Have a question about MusicBrainz?

Please check the General FAQ, or one of the other pages of frequently asked questions, to see if your question has already been answered. If you're using MusicBrainz Picard, check its troubleshooting page.

Data removal requests

MusicBrainz does not edit or remove factual data upon request. Please read our data removal policy for more information.

Copyright issues

If you believe that MusicBrainz violates your copyright, you may report a copyright violation. Please note that MusicBrainz provides data about recordings, not the music itself. If you find your music listed in MusicBrainz it does not mean that we have access to or provide access to the music files themselves. If you still think that we're infringing your copyright, you must provide us with a link to where you can download the infringing material from our website. If you cannot provide us a link, we cannot help you.

Contact details

Please do not contact us with the following topics:

  • We cannot license any of the music listed in MusicBrainz, since we have neither the music itself nor the rights to license the music.
  • We are not distributing any of the music listed in MusicBrainz.
  • We are not interested in your advertising, SEO optimization, or software monetization services. Please do not offer us any services!

If you disregard this agreement and contact us about one of the disallowed topics, we reserve the right to make fun of you.
